Students have the opportunity to program their own Connect 4 AI programs which compete in a tournament moderated by a really shiny program.

# The _Connect 4_ Tournament At the end of this course, selected and interested students will have the opportunity to program their own Connect 4 AI player. The Connect 4 AI's will compete against each other in a Connect 4 tournament that will be held during the night of the LEGO Robot Round-Up. The AI programs will be moderated by a program that will simply tell the AI's what move their opponent made and ask them what move they want to make in response. # Resources * [AI Example Program](http://www.cogsci.rpi.edu/public_html/heuveb/Teaching/M&M/Connect4/ExampleC4AI.cpp) * [Instructions for Using the Moderator Program](http://www.cogsci.rpi.edu/public_html/heuveb/Teaching/M&M/Connect4/Moderator.doc) * [Moderator Program](http://www.cogsci.rpi.edu/public_html/heuveb/Teaching/M&M/Connect4/AIs/c4mod.exe) * [Presentation With Some Basic Strategies](http://www.cogsci.rpi.edu/public_html/heuveb/Teaching/M&M/Connect4/AI%20for%20Connect-4.pptx) * [Instructions for Write Up](http://www.cogsci.rpi.edu/public_html/heuveb/Teaching/M&M/Connect4/Connect4WriteUp.doc)
